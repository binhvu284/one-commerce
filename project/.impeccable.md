## Design Context

### Users
- **Who they are**: B2B Business Owners managing stores, and B2C Customers shopping online.
- **Their context**: Business owners need powerful tools to scale; Customers need fast, frictionless checkout.
- **Job to be done**: Easily create, manage, and shop at custom online stores.

### Brand Personality
- Modern, Professional, Seamless
- Evokes confidence for sellers and trust for buyers.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Visual Tone**: Minimal and elegant with high-contrast, bold typography.
- **Theme**: Light mode primary for admin, full Light/Dark support for customer storefronts.
- **Colors**: Neutral palette (slate/gray) with a vibrant primary accent (e.g., deep blue or brand-specific).

### Design Principles (Impeccable Standards)
1. **Ruthless Simplicity**: No cluttered interfaces; actions should be immediately obvious.
2. **Typography First**: Use precise typography scales (Google Fonts like Inter or Geist) for hierarchical clarity.
3. **Subtle Delight**: Seamless micro-animations on interaction (buttons, navs, dialogs).
4. **Predictability**: Consistent component states across the entire application.
5. **Accessibility**: High contrast ratios and full keyboard navigation compliance.
