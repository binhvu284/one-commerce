\# Features \& Scope Guide



This document provides a detailed breakdown of all features included in your ecommerce platform, organized by functional area.



---



\## 1. Product Catalog Management



\### What is it?

The product catalog is the heart of your store. It's where all your merchandise is organized, described, and made available for customers to discover and purchase.



\### Features Included



\*\*Product Information\*\*

\- Add, edit, and organize products with detailed descriptions

\- Upload multiple product images and thumbnails

\- Organize products into categories and subcategories for easy navigation

\- Add product specifications and attributes (material, color, size, etc.)

\- Set product visibility (draft, published, or archived)



\*\*Product Variants\*\*

\- Create variants for different options (e.g., sizes S, M, L or colors red, blue, green)

\- Set different prices for each variant

\- Manage inventory levels per variant

\- Track which variants are in stock or out of stock



\*\*Pricing \& Inventory\*\*

\- Set base prices and compare-at prices (for sale displays)

\- Apply currency-specific pricing for different regions

\- Real-time inventory tracking

\- Low-stock alerts and notifications

\- Automatic inventory deduction when orders are placed



\### What Customers See

\- Product listings with images and descriptions

\- Available variants to choose from

\- Current pricing and stock status ("In Stock" or "Out of Stock")

\- Product reviews and ratings (if enabled in future versions)



---



\## 2. Shopping Cart \& Checkout



\### What is it?

The checkout experience is where customers finalize their purchases. A smooth, secure checkout reduces abandoned carts and increases sales.



\### Features Included



\*\*Shopping Cart\*\*

\- Add and remove products from cart

\- Update product quantities

\- View real-time pricing and totals

\- Persistent cart (saved between sessions)

\- Apply coupon codes or discount codes



\*\*Checkout Process\*\*

\- Multi-step checkout flow (review cart → shipping → payment → confirmation)

\- Collect customer shipping and billing addresses

\- Validate addresses for accuracy

\- Select preferred shipping method and view shipping costs

\- Review order summary before final payment

\- Secure payment processing



\*\*Order Confirmation\*\*

\- Instant order confirmation on screen

\- Confirmation email sent to customer with order details

\- Order number for tracking and reference



---



\## 3. Customer Management



\### What is it?

Customers are the foundation of your business. This system manages customer data, accounts, and their relationship with your store.



\### Features Included



\*\*Customer Accounts\*\*

\- Customer registration and email verification

\- Secure login and password management

\- Profile information (name, email, phone)

\- Multiple saved addresses (home, office, etc.)

\- Order history and past purchases

\- Account settings and communication preferences



\*\*Customer Communications\*\*

\- Order status updates via email

\- Shipping notifications with tracking information

\- Promotional email opt-in/opt-out

\- Account security notifications



\*\*For Your Admin Team\*\*

\- View all customer profiles

\- Search and filter customers by name, email, or order history

\- See customer lifetime value and purchase behavior

\- Manage customer account status



---



\## 4. Orders \& Fulfillment



\### What is it?

Order management is how your team processes, tracks, and fulfills customer purchases from start to finish.



\### Features Included



\*\*Order Lifecycle\*\*

\- Automatic order creation when customer completes checkout

\- Order status tracking (Pending → Confirmed → Processing → Shipped → Delivered)

\- Order notes and internal comments for your team

\- Order cancellation (with conditions)



\*\*Order Details Visible to Admin\*\*

\- Complete order information (items, quantities, prices)

\- Customer contact details

\- Shipping address

\- Payment method and payment status

\- Shipping method selected



\*\*For Your Customers\*\*

\- Order confirmation immediately after purchase

\- Ability to view order status in their account

\- Email notifications when order ships

\- Tracking number and estimated delivery date (if carrier provides)

\- Contact info for support inquiries



\*\*Invoicing \& Documentation\*\*

\- Automatic invoice generation

\- Invoice download and email options

\- Print-ready order receipts



---



\## 5. Payments \& Payment Processing



\### What is it?

Payment processing handles all money transactions securely and compliantly, protecting both your business and your customers.



\### Features Included



\*\*Payment Gateway Integration\*\*

\- Stripe integration (primary payment processor)

\- Support for credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, etc.)

\- Multiple payment methods support (additional gateways can be added)

\- Secure, PCI-compliant payment processing



\*\*Payment Status Management\*\*

\- Automatic payment verification

\- Payment success/failure notifications to customer and store

\- Failed payment retry options

\- Refund processing capability



\*\*Security\*\*

\- All payment data encrypted in transit

\- No storage of full credit card details in your system

\- Compliance with PCI Data Security Standard (PCI DSS)



---



\## 6. Shipping \& Delivery



\### What is it?

Shipping management ensures customers receive their orders reliably and know when to expect delivery.



\### Features Included



\*\*Shipping Configuration\*\*

\- Define shipping zones (e.g., local, national, international)

\- Set up multiple shipping methods (Standard, Express, Overnight, etc.)

\- Configure shipping costs per method and zone

\- Free shipping thresholds (e.g., free shipping on orders over $50)



\*\*Shipping Integration\*\*

\- Integration with major carrier APIs (optional, can be added)

\- Automatic shipping label generation

\- Tracking number assignment and communication

\- Delivery status updates



\*\*For Customers\*\*

\- Available shipping methods shown at checkout with costs

\- Estimated delivery dates

\- Tracking numbers in order confirmation and status updates

\- Easy tracking link to follow package



---



\## 7. Discounts \& Promotions



\### What is it?

Promotions drive sales by offering customers special deals and incentives while helping you manage margins.



\### Features Included



\*\*Discount Types\*\*

\- Percentage-based discounts (e.g., 20% off)

\- Fixed amount discounts (e.g., $10 off)

\- Free shipping offers

\- Buy-one-get-one (BOGO) offers (subject to configuration)



\*\*Coupon Codes\*\*

\- Create and manage coupon/discount codes

\- Set expiration dates

\- Limit usage (e.g., one per customer, or max 100 uses total)

\- Minimum purchase requirements (e.g., "$50 minimum purchase")

\- Specific product eligibility (apply to certain products only)

\- Customers apply codes at checkout



\*\*Seasonal \& Promotional Campaigns\*\*

\- Time-limited promotions (e.g., Black Friday sale)

\- Automatic application of promotions matching criteria

\- Track promotion usage and effectiveness



\*\*For Your Customers\*\*

\- See discount applied in cart and checkout

\- Clear display of savings

\- Error messages if code is invalid or expired

\## 8. Admin Dashboard



\### What is it?

The Admin Dashboard is your command center—the web interface where your team manages the entire store.



\### Features Included



\*\*Dashboard Overview\*\*

\- Sales summary (total revenue, number of orders today)

\- Recent orders list

\- Popular products

\- Quick-access shortcuts to main functions



\*\*Product Management\*\*

\- Full product creation, editing, and deletion

\- Bulk import/export (when needed)

\- Variant management

\- Inventory management

\- Product categorization



\*\*Order Management\*\*

\- View all orders with filters and search

\- Update order status

\- Print invoices and packing slips

\- Manage refunds

\- Add internal notes



\*\*Customer Management\*\*

\- Customer directory and search

\- View customer profiles and order history

\- Update customer information

\- Manage customer segments (optional, future)



\*\*Store Configuration\*\*

\- General store settings (name, contact info, branding)

\- Payment method configuration

\- Shipping zone and method setup

\- Tax rate configuration

\- Currency and regional settings

\- Store policies and legal text



\*\*Reporting \& Analytics\*\* \*(Basic in v1)\*

\- Sales reports (revenue by time period)

\- Product performance (best sellers, slow movers)

\- Customer insights (top customers, repeat purchase rate)

\- Discount/coupon effectiveness

\## 9. Security \& Data Protection



\### What is it?

Security protections ensure your business data and customer information are safe from unauthorized access.



\### Features Included



\*\*User Access Control\*\*

\- Admin login with email and secure password

\- Different permission levels (staff vs. manager vs. owner)

\- Session management and automatic logout

\- Activity logs for audit trails



\*\*Data Protection\*\*

\- Encrypted connections (HTTPS/SSL) for all customer interactions

\- Encrypted storage of sensitive customer data

\- Regular backups of all store data

\- Compliance with data protection regulations



\*\*Customer Data Security\*\*

\- Secure password hashing

\- PCI DSS compliance for payment data

\- GDPR compliance options (customer data export, deletion requests)

\### Features Included
