\# Ecommerce System Overview



\## What Are We Building?



We are building a custom ecommerce platform tailored specifically for your brand. This system enables your customers to browse products, add items to their cart, complete purchases, and track their orders—all through a modern, fast, and reliable online storefront.



The platform is built on \*\*Vendure\*\*, an open-source headless commerce framework. "Headless" means the backend (which handles all the business logic) is completely separate from the frontend (which customers see). This separation gives us flexibility: we can build a beautiful storefront optimized for your brand, while the backend handles orders, payments, inventory, and customer data securely and efficiently.



\## Three Main Parts of Your System



\### 1. \*\*Storefront\*\* (What Customers See)

The online shop where your customers browse products, add items to their cart, proceed through checkout, and complete purchases. This is built with React and optimized for mobile and desktop experience. Every interaction here is fast and secure.



\### 2. \*\*API Backend\*\* (The Engine)

The "brain" of the system running on Vendure. It manages:

\- \*\*Product catalog\*\*: All product details, variants (sizes, colors), pricing, images, and inventory

\- \*\*Customers\*\*: Registration, login, profile, order history, and preferences

\- \*\*Orders \& Fulfillment\*\*: Order creation, payment processing, shipment tracking

\- \*\*Payments \& Shipping\*\*: Integration with payment gateways (Stripe, PayPal, etc.) and shipping providers

\- \*\*Discounts \& Promotions\*\*: Coupon codes, seasonal sales, and promotional offers

\- \*\*Settings \& Configuration\*\*: Store branding, tax rules, currencies, regions



\### 3. \*\*Admin Dashboard\*\* (Where Your Team Works)

A secure management interface where your staff can:

\- \*\*Manage Products\*\*: Add, edit, or remove products; manage variants and inventory levels

\- \*\*View \& Process Orders\*\*: Monitor incoming orders, update order status, generate invoices, arrange shipments

\- \*\*Manage Customers\*\*: View customer profiles, order history, and communication preferences

\- \*\*Configure Store Settings\*\*: Update branding, payment methods, shipping zones, tax rates, and promotions

\- \*\*View Reports \& Analytics\*\*: Track sales, popular products, customer trends (scope permitting)



\## Who Uses What?



| User Type | What They Access | Main Tasks |

|-----------|-----------------|-----------|

| \*\*Customers\*\* | Storefront website | Browse, search, add to cart, checkout, track orders |

| \*\*Store Staff\*\* | Admin Dashboard | Manage inventory, process orders, update order status |

| \*\*Store Manager / Owner\*\* | Admin Dashboard + Reports | Monitor sales, set promotions, configure store, make decisions |
